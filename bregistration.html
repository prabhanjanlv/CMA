Click Here for New Member Registration

1. Registration
  • Click on the new member registration link
  • Enter information as requested for parents and children who will be attending Bala Vihar
  • Click on Submit Registration button
2. Confirm Registration
  • Verify information that you have entered and confirm registration
  • To Update, go back to the registration form and submit updates
  • This will take you to the payment page
3. Payment Information
  • Your payment totals are calculated based on the language classes you have registered for
  • Click on Donate. This will now take you to PayPal, a secure site, to pay your Bala Vihar donation by credit card
  • Fill in all fields as instructed by PayPal and make your payment
4. Confirmation
  • After completing your payment, you will be taken to a confirmation page
  • You will also get an email from PayPal confirming your payment
  • Please save this email as your proof of payment

Click Here for Returning Member Registration

1. Verification
  • Click on returning member registration link
  • Enter information for one of the children who attended Bala Vihar. Click on Verify
  • If your information is found, it will be displayed. Now click on the "Complete Registration and Payment" link to continue with registration
  • If your information is not found, you can try again or use the new member registration link
2. Registration
  • For each of your children to be registered, click on the "Register Now" link in Status
  • In the Children Information section below, update information as necessary
  • Select Hindi to register for Hindi
  • Select Confirm Registration checkbox and then click on Verify and Update Child Information to complete updates
  • Add another child if applicable
3. Parent Information
  • Update parent information section if necessary
  • Click on Save Changes
4. Payment Information
  • Your payment totals are calculated based on the language classes you have registered for
  • Click on Donate. This will now take you to PayPal, a secure site, to pay your Bala Vihar donation by credit card
  • Fill in all fields as instructed by PayPal and make your payment

Refund Policy: No refund after 30 days of start of session
